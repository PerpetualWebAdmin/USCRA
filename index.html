University, College and School Registars Association

The University, College, and School Registrars Association (UCSRA), Inc. is the official association of the registrars
and records personnel in Region IV–A. The UCSRA is a vital organization that empowers professionals working
in registrar offices to excel in their roles. By fostering collaboration, providing educational resources, and
advocating for service excellence, UCSRA plays a crucial part in ensuring the effective administration of educational
institutions regarding student records management.
